I.   Gravity

I never planned to look twice
but you exist the way the moon exists,
effortless, pulling tides in me
I didn't know I had.

II.   Your Names

Eniola — honour wrapped in breath,
a name that means the world gave her wealth
before she even knew to ask.

Fifehanmi — God expanded my joy.
I believe it.
I felt it the day I met her.

Arike — one who is cared for
by all who behold her.
That one, I've seen myself.

Fatiah — the opening.
She walks into a room
and something unlocks.

And Estrella — my name for her,
the one I whisper when the sky is full
because she is all of these things,
and she is also my star.

III.   Today, You

If I could hold a moment still,
it would be this one
the day the world was given you.

Not just your smile,
not just your voice,
but the quiet way you make
everything feel lighter around you.

You are the kind of warmth
people search for in places,
not knowing it exists
in a person like you.

And today,
I don't just celebrate your birth
I celebrate the way you found me,
and somehow made life softer.

Happy Birthday, my love.
